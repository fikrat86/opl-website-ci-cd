About Us

The Oshawa Public Library has been a cornerstone of the community since its establishment in 1864. Our mission is to foster a love of reading and learning, and to provide a welcoming space for all members of the community.

Services

  • Book and media lending
  • Public computers and Wi-Fi
  • Children's programs
  • Adult literacy programs
  • Meeting rooms for public use
